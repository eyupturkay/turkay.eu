---
question: "How does remote software development work?"
order: 2
---
Remote development follows a structured process: free discovery call, detailed proposal, development in 1-2 week sprints with live demos after each sprint, comprehensive testing, and deployment. Communication happens via WhatsApp/Slack for instant messaging, weekly status reports, and video calls for sprint reviews. All source code, documentation, and access credentials are delivered to you at project completion.
