The gap between AI hype and AI reality continues to narrow. In 2025, we're seeing enterprises move beyond pilot projects into production-scale AI deployments that genuinely transform operations.

## What's Working

**Document Processing & Extraction** remains the highest-ROI use case. Companies processing thousands of invoices, contracts, or forms daily are seeing 70-80% cost reductions with modern document AI.

**Predictive Maintenance** in manufacturing and logistics has matured significantly. Our clients report 30-40% reductions in unplanned downtime.

**Customer Service Automation** using LLMs has finally crossed the quality threshold. The key insight: hybrid approaches where AI handles routine queries and escalates complex ones to humans.

## What's Not

Fully autonomous decision-making systems remain problematic in regulated industries. The most successful implementations keep humans in the loop for critical decisions.

## Our Recommendation

Start with high-volume, repetitive processes where accuracy can be measured objectively. Build confidence and expertise before tackling more complex use cases.
